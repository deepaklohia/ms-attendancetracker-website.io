Time Saving Attendance Tracker can be very helpful for your employees / students or anyone where you want to keep track of attendance .


STEP1: Click on “Fill Header” button , it will automatically fill date from first to last of the selected month.



STEP2: Enter employee/ students name in the column A/B .



STEP3: Click on Auto-Fill to mark attendance automatically for the entire month for the names entered.



Change the Week attendance type , for example “P” , “PL from the dropdown to mark precise attendance. Select a range and Mark attendance for selected items. 



you can records upto 5000 employee / student records as of now.
